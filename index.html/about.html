About Our Initiative

1. Introduction

This website is built to promote awareness and responsibility towards the maintenance and preservation of our forts. It serves as a digital bridge between visitors and caretakers, allowing people to stay informed and take action for heritage protection.

2. Our Work

Our main goal is to make it easier to report any issue related to fort conditions — whether it’s damage, pollution, or lack of cleanliness. Through active participation, everyone can contribute to preserving the beauty and integrity of these historical sites.

3. Clean Environment Reflects Clean Choices

A well-maintained fort is a reflection of collective care and respect for history. Keeping surroundings clean not only protects the structure but also inspires visitors to make more conscious, eco-friendly choices that support sustainable tourism.

Preserving history through clean and responsible choices.
