Forts of Maharashtra

Raigad Fort

The capital of the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Known for its strategic height and historic architecture.

Purandar Fort

A fort with panoramic views and deep historical roots, where the Treaty of Purandar was signed in 1665.

Sindhudurg Fort

Built on an island in the Arabian Sea by Shivaji Maharaj, this sea fort stands as a marvel of coastal defense.

Panhala Fort

One of the largest forts in the Deccan, Panhala played a key role in Maratha history and offers scenic hill views.

Pratapgad Fort

Famous for the battle between Shivaji Maharaj and Afzal Khan, this fort symbolizes courage and valor.

Murud-Janjira Fort

An undefeated sea fort, surrounded by the Arabian Sea, known for its unique construction and history of resistance.
