A Smart Fort Protection & Reporting System

Maintaining cleanliness at historical forts is crucial for preserving our shared legacy and history.

Raigad Fort

The capital fort of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, known for its stunning view and deep historical significance.

Purandar Fort

Located near Pune, this fort played a vital role in the history of the Maratha Empire and its military strategy.

Sindhudurg Fort

Built on an island in the Arabian Sea, Sindhudurg showcases Shivaji Maharaj’s naval vision and architectural genius.

Panhala Fort

One of the largest forts in Maharashtra, offering a glimpse into the Maratha dynasty’s strong defense history.

Pratapgad Fort

Famous for the legendary battle between Shivaji Maharaj and Afzal Khan, this fort stands as a symbol of bravery.

Murud-Janjira Fort

Built on a rocky island, Janjira remained unconquered for centuries, reflecting exceptional maritime strength.

Sinhagad Fort

Known for the valor of Tanaji Malusare, Sinhagad stands as a symbol of courage and sacrifice near Pune.

Shivneri Fort

The birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, Shivneri Fort represents the foundation of Maratha pride and legacy.
